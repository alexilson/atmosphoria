Welcome to Atmosphoria!

Atmosphoria is the first weather app designed from the ground-up to give you a customized forecast in the voice of your choice.

Simply enter the zip code for the area you want to forecast, select your favorite accent, and click button.

In seconds, you'll be whisked away to another page that will display your customized forecast.

You'll also be able to see the last 5 responses, giving you a history so you can see all the fun from the past.

Enjoy!
